# MATHBHOOT Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: 19 July 2026

MATHBHOOT is currently a static website. It does not provide user accounts or accept personal-information submissions through the site.

## Information we process

- Basic technical and analytics data may be processed when analytics is enabled, such as page views, device type, and approximate location.
- Usage data: device/browser information, pages visited, and analytics information where analytics is enabled.
- Voluntary data: recruitment ideas, support messages, and other material a person chooses to submit.

Any future account, profile, or submission feature will require an updated policy before it launches.

## Why we process information

We use limited site data to understand website performance, maintain security, respond to support or legal requests, and improve MATHBHOOT. We do not sell personal information.

## Age limitation

MATHBHOOT does not currently offer account registration or personal-information forms.

## Service providers and transfers

MATHBHOOT uses Vercel for hosting and Google Analytics where configured. These providers may process limited technical data under their applicable terms and data-protection arrangements.

## Retention and security

MATHBHOOT does not retain account information because accounts are not currently available. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Your choices and rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information that may be held through support contact or analytics, subject to identity verification and applicable law.

## Changes and contact

Material changes will receive a new policy version and, where appropriate, renewed user acknowledgement. Privacy or rights requests should be sent through the official contact method published on `https://mathbhoot.com`.
