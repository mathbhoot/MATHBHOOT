# MATHBHOOT Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: 19 July 2026

MATHBHOOT collects only the information reasonably needed to operate accounts, protect the service, and provide its mathematics-focused experiences. This policy describes MATHBHOOT's responsibilities; Supabase and other providers have separate policies that govern their own services.

## Information we process

- Account data: name, date of birth, email address, authentication identifiers, policy-acceptance records, and account status.
- Security data: CAPTCHA results, authentication events, abuse-prevention signals, and technical logs.
- Usage data: device/browser information, pages visited, and analytics information where analytics is enabled.
- Voluntary data: recruitment ideas, support messages, and other material a person chooses to submit.

Name, date of birth, and email are private account data and are not intended for public display. Future public usernames, profiles, posts, videos, animations, code, and other creator content will be governed by an updated policy before those features launch.

## Why we process information

We process information to create and secure accounts, verify eligibility, provide requested features, prevent fraud and abuse, respond to support or legal requests, enforce our Terms, and improve MATHBHOOT. We do not sell personal information.

## Age limitation

Account registration is currently limited to people aged 18 or older. MATHBHOOT will not intentionally enable accounts for minors until an appropriate guardian-consent and child-safety process has been designed and reviewed.

## Service providers and transfers

MATHBHOOT uses service providers including Supabase for authentication and database services, Cloudflare Turnstile for abuse prevention, Vercel for hosting, and Google Analytics where configured. These providers may process data in other countries under their applicable terms and data-protection arrangements.

## Retention and security

We retain account information while an account remains active and afterward only as reasonably required for deletion processing, security, dispute resolution, or legal obligations. Access controls, row-level security, encrypted transport, and restricted administrative access are used to reduce risk, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Your choices and rights

Users may request access, correction, export, or deletion of their personal information, subject to identity verification and applicable law. A signed-in user may submit an account-deletion request from the account interface. Some records may be retained where legally required or necessary to investigate abuse or resolve disputes.

## Changes and contact

Material changes will receive a new policy version and, where appropriate, renewed user acknowledgement. Privacy or rights requests should be sent through the official contact method published on `https://mathbhoot.com`.
