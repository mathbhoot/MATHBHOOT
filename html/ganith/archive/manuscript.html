Rules, hints, and hidden instructions that live inside the story instead of outside it.

Inside the Story

The manuscript page is for rules that appear naturally inside the narrative: warnings, puzzle hints, limitations, rewards, and conditions that guide the reader or player.

Suggested Manuscript Fields

  • Rule title
  • Story line or clue source
  • Condition or time limit
  • Result if the rule is followed or broken
