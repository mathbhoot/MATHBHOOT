ANSH

WHERE NUMBERS COME ALIVE AND SO DO THE NIGHTMARES.

Meet Ansh, the naughtiest student in St. Xavier's history the chaos conductor, the legend of every hostel corridor. But at midnight, under a blanket with a torch and a mathematics textbook, he is something else entirely: focused, hungry, terrified of being ordinary, and completely unaware that something inside the school walls has been watching him for a very long time waiting for exactly the kind of boy who laughs the loudest, hides the deepest, and walks through doors that say, "Enter At Your Own Risk."
Ansh is the first protagonist of the MATHBHOOT series.
His curiosity leads him into a world where mathematics is no longer just a theory. It is alive.
